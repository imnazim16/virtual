NDIS Services

Innovative mental wellbeing “Virtual Psychologist” goes beyond conventional therapeutic support, offering a comprehensive range of services, including independent psychosocial recovery coaching, coordination, and counselling. These services are designed to assist participants in living life to its fullest.

Know more
Arrow
Virtual Wellness Program

We believe in partnering with individuals who have a psychosocial disability, working closely with them to help them get closer to their goals and build a life that transcends the constraints of a diagnosis. Our approach is rooted in the idea that life should not be defined by one’s diagnosis.

Our dedicated practitioners work individually with NDIS participants, guiding them towards their desired outcomes while also facilitating self-knowledge, emotional acceptance, and the development of personal resources. By partnering with participants on their journey to self-discovery and personal growth, we aim to create an environment that nurtures their emotional and mental well-being.

In addition to traditional therapeutic support, we provide independent psychosocial recovery coaching and counselling to help participants regain control of their lives and work towards fulfilling their potential. Our collaborative approach ensures that participants can gain greater insight into their own lives, leading to a more fulfilling and balanced existence, irrespective of their impairment.
