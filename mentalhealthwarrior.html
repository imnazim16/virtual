Mental Health Warrior

We imagine a world where anyone can access hope anytime, anywhere. MentalHealthWarrior supports this mission by connecting people through an ecommerce platform. It is targeted towards youth and students who prefer Text based channels and aims to support their development, education and relationships in home and school. The site also offers a clothing range that brings about awareness for mental health in the youth sector.

How it works

Simply visit the “shop” section of the website and select “Text counselling sessions” and add to cart. Proceed to check out and make payment. Once paid, a link will be sent to you to set up your profile and book an appointment from a calendar to engage with a Mental Health Practitioner. The one- hour real time session via Text will be initiated by the practitioner at the time and date of your appointment.

Benefits

Text counselling is non confronting, convenient, accessible, and easy to use with a very strong social impact that allows users to reflect on their conversations, improve their social wellbeing, bi pass wait times for better availability and provide CALD users with an easier mode for communication.
